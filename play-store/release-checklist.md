# Google Play Release Checklist

- Create or confirm the Play Console app with package name `com.termkey.ime`
- Upload a signed `app-release.aab`
- Enable Play App Signing
- Fill in app category, contact email, and support details
- Publish a public privacy policy URL based on `docs/privacy-policy.md`
- Complete Data safety for:
  - microphone audio for optional voice input
  - app functionality purpose
  - no developer-operated backend for typed text
- Complete app access, ads, content rating, and target audience forms
- Review store listing text in `play-store/listing-en-US.md` and `play-store/listing-zh-CN.md`
- Replace generated screenshots with real device screenshots if needed before production rollout
